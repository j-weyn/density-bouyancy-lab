Project Information

About

Fluid Mechanics Learning Hub

This interactive learning platform is designed to help students understand the fundamental concepts of fluid mechanics through hands-on simulations, visualizations, and assessments. Whether you're exploring density calculations or buoyancy principles, our comprehensive lab environment provides an engaging way to master fluid mechanics concepts.

About This App

The Fluid Mechanics Learning Hub was created by a team of dedicated students to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical understanding. This project combines interactive simulations with assessment tools to create a complete learning experience for fluid mechanics education.

Key Features

  • Density Simulation: Calculate object density using mass and volume measurements with real-time visualization.
  • Buoyancy Test: Observe and predict how objects float or sink in different fluids based on density comparison.
  • Interactive Quiz: Test your understanding with simulation-integrated questions and track your progress.
  • Professor Dashboard: Instructors can view detailed student records, quiz results, and generate reports.
  • Single-Attempt Assessment: Students take the quiz once per session, ensuring focused, intentional learning.

Concepts Covered

  • Density and Mass-Volume Relationships
  • Buoyancy and Archimedes' Principle
  • Fluid Properties and Behavior
  • Pressure and Depth Calculations
  • Floating and Sinking Conditions
  • Real-World Applications of Fluid Mechanics

Getting Started

For Students: Head to the Simulation section to begin the tutorial, explore the density and buoyancy labs, and take the assessment quiz.

For Professors: Use the Admin Login to access the dashboard, view all student submissions, and download records for further analysis.
