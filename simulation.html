Fluid Mechanics Info

Fluid Mechanics Guide

Fluid mechanics studies how liquids and gases behave. In this simulation, water is the fluid. You will calculate density first, then use buoyancy to predict if the object floats or sinks.

Meaning

Fluid mechanics explains how water pushes on objects and how an object's density affects its motion in the fluid.

Density Formula

ρ = m / V

Density equals mass divided by volume.

Buoyancy Formula

Fb = ρwatergV

Buoyant force depends on water density, gravity, and displaced volume.

Water Rule

Fresh water has a density of 1000 kg/m³. Lower density floats. Higher density sinks.
